Delete Your Account

Applies to the TripShare Atlas Android application

This page explains how to request deletion of your TripShare Atlas account and the data associated with it. You can do this yourself inside the app, or by contacting the developer.

1.Delete your account from inside the app

This is the fastest way and is available to every user:

  • Open the TripShare Atlas app and sign in.
  • Go to Profile (the profile icon in the app).
  • Tap Delete Account.
  • Confirm your identity when prompted (re-enter your password).
  • If you are the sole admin of any trips, the app will guide you to either hand those trips to another member or delete them.
  • Confirm the final prompt. Your account is then deleted.

2.Delete your account by email

If you cannot access the app, email the developer at divyanshuprakash780@gmail.com from the email address registered to your account, with the subject "Delete my account". Your request will be actioned and your account deleted, normally within 7 days.

3.What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following is permanently removed:

  • Your authentication record (sign-in credentials).
  • Your personal profile data — display name, email address, avatar, and currency preference.

4.What data is kept

Expenses, settlements, and activity entries that you added to a shared trip may remain visible to the other members of that trip. This is necessary so the remaining members' balances stay accurate and fair. This retained content is no longer linked to an active account or to your personal profile.

Anonymous diagnostic and crash data already collected is not personally identifiable and may be retained for up to 90 days before it is purged.

Tip: if you want all of your trip data gone as well, remove your expenses or leave/delete your trips before deleting your account.

5.Need help?

For any question about account or data deletion, contact the developer at divyanshuprakash780@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
